## 📝 Tips for Recording

1. **Pace:** Speak naturally, not too fast. Pause 2-3 seconds between actions.
2. **Energy:** Keep it enthusiastic but professional. You're showing something impressive.
3. **Mistakes:** If you make a small error, just continue. It shows the software is real.
4. **Screen:** Use 1080p or higher. Zoom into relevant areas when needed.
5. **Music:** Light background music (no lyrics) during intro and transitions.
6. **Length:** Edit final video to 15-20 minutes. Remove dead time between actions.
7. **CTA:** End with contact information and next steps.
